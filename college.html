About sherubtse College

Sherubtse College, established in 1978, is one of the premier higher education institutions in Bhutan, located in the eastern region of the country in Kanglung, Trashigang. Initially founded as a school to promote higher education in the region, it was upgraded to a college in 1998 and is now affiliated with the Royal University of Bhutan

Mission & Vision of Sherubtse College

Mission

To provide GNH inspired quality tertiary education in Liberal Arts and Sciences. To contribute to the development of knowledge-based society through knowledge creation, sharing and advocacy

Vision

An internationally recognized institute in Liberal Arts and Sciences with an emphasis on GNH value based learning
