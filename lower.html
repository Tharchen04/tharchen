About Tshaphel lower Secondary school

Tshaphel Lower Secondary School is a vibrant educational institution located in the beautiful valley of Haa, Bhutan. The school serves students from classes Pre-Primary (PP) through class 8, providing a strong foundation in academics, values, and life skills.

Mission & Vision of Tshaphel lower Secondary school

Mission

To create a school where every student grows into a confident, kind, and curious person, ready to succeed and make a positive difference in the world.

Vision

Prepare students with skills and knowledge to face challenges and achieve their goals in life.
