About Sangbaykha Primary School

Sangbaykha Primary School is dedicated to nurturing the talents and skills of every child, promoting an inclusive and engaging environment for students to learn and grow. It is located in one of the remote villages under Haa Dzongkhag. I have studied till class 4 where I moved to TLSS under certain circumstances.

Mission & Vision of Sangbaykha Primary School

Mission

To provide quality education that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and moral values among students.

Vision

To be a model primary school that prepares students to become confident, responsible, and lifelong learners.
