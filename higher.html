About Gongzim Ugyen Dorji Central School

Gongzim Ugyen Dorji Central School, established during the reign of the Third King of Bhutan, Jigme Dorji Wangchuck, is recognized as the first-ever school in the country. Founded by Gongzim Ugyen Dorji in 1950, it marked a significant milestone in Bhutan's educational history, laying the foundation for modern education in the nation.

Mission & Vision of Gongzim Ugyen Dorji Central School

Mission

To provide a holistic and quality education that nurtures the intellectual, emotional, and social development of students.

Vision

The school envisions creating a nurturing and inclusive environment where every student is empowered to reach their full potential, equipped with the knowledge and skills necessary for success in a rapidly changing world while preserving the rich cultural heritage of Bhutan.
